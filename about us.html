ABOUT CAP

We are not an impersonal talent agency, but rather a large group of talented and professional entertainers dedicated to giving consistent, quality performances. When you want the finest entertainment for your next special occasion, let us arrange it. We can provide you with the best elegant & exciting bands, musicians, DJ's, dancers and much more.

Ron Nathan has shared the stage with noted musicians such as; Jose Feliciano, The Platters, Gladys Knight, Stevie Wonder, and Stan Kenton, to name but a few. Four times on the Tonight Show and spots on the Merv Griffen, John Davidson, Toni Tennielle, and many other broadcasts have earned him notoriety among his peers. Ron is well versed in all styles of music and takes pride in his showmanship
